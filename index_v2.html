The Science

How Scent Rewires
the Workplace Brain

01
Molecules Enter the Nasal Passage
Aromatic compounds contact the olfactory epithelium the only sensory system with a direct neural pathway to the brain's emotional center, bypassing the rational cortex entirely.
02
Olfactory Bulb Signals the Limbic System
Signals travel in milliseconds to the limbic system the brain's command center for emotion, memory and stress regulation. No other sense has this direct access.
03
Amygdala & Hippocampus Respond
Stress hormones like cortisol and adrenaline are regulated. The amygdala's threat response calms. Emotional processing shifts to a more balanced, productive state.
04
Measurable Physiological Change
Heart rate lowers. Cortisol drops. Focus sharpens. The nervous system shifts from fight-or-flight to calm, high-performance within minutes, not weeks.
